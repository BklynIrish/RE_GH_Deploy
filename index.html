Renewable Energy Consumption in the US

The objective of this project was to find particular data on a topic of interest and to use different web development software to create a variety of visualizations that will tell a story through using data in our database on Renewable Energy Resource Consumption in the US.

We have become more and more aware of the need to care for our environment therefore Renewable Energy Resources are becoming of greater interest, prevalence, and use. The effect of some types of conventional energy sources, such as fossil fuels are being shown to have had and continue to have a negative impact on controversial topics such as Global Warming and the effect that such traditional, less clean, and limited energy sources have on slowly deteriorating have on our slowly deteriorating ozone layer and, hence, the quality of the air we breathe on a daily basis.

Solar Power, Wind Power, and Hydro Power/Hydroelectricity are the major three sources of RE (Renewable Energy). Hence, we will look particularly at theses three types of energy and how much of these we are using through a series of visualizations. The fact alone that these resources are renewable by natural forces is of integral importance to why they are so important.
